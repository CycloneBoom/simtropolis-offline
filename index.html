Statement from Dirktator, CB, and Cori

We will be back, but...

Unfortunately, as we've learned more about the hosting situation, things have taken a sharp turn for the worse.

Due to an aggressive policy following a billing error, the host has wiped the server!

Dirk informed us of this bad news, in what was totally unexpected and a worst case scenario. This follows what we believed was a positive outcome, as we thought the hosting was merely suspended (not terminated at short notice).

However, we need to emphasise how it does NOT mean the end of Simtropolis. We repeat, this is NOT the end of Simtropolis.

What this means is it will take longer to rebuild what we had, and recover what will be missing.

Thankfully we have a full database backup from March, (STEX file entries, forum posts, CJs, etc) along with the board software files and custom modifications all secured. Additionally, we have all user uploaded content (STEX, attachments, etc) up to June 2024, which means ~90% of the site's history is safe. As of writing this, the backups are being transferred to secure external storage.

Once the site returns, we'll be developing a fast-tracked initiative to restore all STEX files after this point, and recruiting volunteers who wish to help with the recovery efforts. We will appreciate the help from all members who can assist in any capacity.

This will not be an easy task, but the SC4 community does not end here. This has been a sudden disaster which has swept through our Simtropolis town, but we will rebuild with a positive purpose.

We understand a lot of you will be disappointed and even angry at this outcome. We're not pleased about it either in the slightest. Please bear with us as we pursue all possible options to restore the site back as we know it.

We'll keep you updated via this message as things develop.

Simtropolis Will Return

-CB & Cori
